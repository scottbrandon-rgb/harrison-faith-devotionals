Last week we learned to lament when God feels hidden. This week we ask the question underneath the ache, is emptiness a sign of failure? Because if it is, we have to explain the emptiest man who ever lived, the One who emptied Himself in obedience all the way to a cross. We walk from the drink offering poured out beside the altar to three faces that show us what God is doing when He drains His people. Jesus shows us qualification. Joseph shows us maturation. Elijah shows us transition. And by Friday you will have the one diagnostic question to pray over your own empty season.
