WEEK OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This week is for anyone running on empty, and given the season our church is walking through, that may be more of us than will admit it. We take Psalm 13 one honest step at a time, from a man after God's own heart asking how long, to the turn where he stops talking about God and starts talking to Him, to the single word "but" that carries a lament all the way over into trust. Then we go to the cross, where the Father truly hid His face from the Son so He would never hide it from us, and we end with Paul, who tells us why God lets His own children run this empty at all. If you are in a dark place right now, this week is my heart to you.

Day 1 — How Long
Day 2 — Eyes Before Exits
Day 3 — Preach to Your Heart
Day 4 — The Face Truly Hidden
Day 5 — Nothing Wasted

You have permission to be honest with God this week, and a whole body walking it beside you. See you in the devos.
