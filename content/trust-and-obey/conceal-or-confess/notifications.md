Series announcement:
This week we are walking the road of Joshua 7 one step at a time, the road Achan took from a single look to a buried secret to a valley named for his trouble. We are tracing how disobedience gets chosen, how mercy keeps narrowing in to give us a way out, and how the grave at the end of the road becomes a door of hope in Christ. Five days, five steps:

Day 1: The First Look
Day 2: The Price Tag
Day 3: The Reach
Day 4: Mercy Narrows In
Day 5: The Grave Becomes a Door

The challenge this week is simple and hard: stop hiding the one thing you have kept buried, and bring it into the light while the door is still open. Conceal, or confess. You get to choose the second half of the verse Achan only lived the first half of. See you in the devos.
