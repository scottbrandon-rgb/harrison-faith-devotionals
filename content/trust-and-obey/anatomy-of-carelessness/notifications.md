Series announcement

This week in Trust and Obey we sit down in Joshua 7, the morning after Jericho, where one man's hidden sin brings a whole nation to a halt. We trace the anatomy of carelessness, three marks that father each other in order: pride, then presumption, then prayerlessness. Then we follow the buried thing all the way to the cross, where Jesus carried out into the open the very thing we keep trying to hide.

The five days:
Day 1: The Only Thing You Bring
Day 2: The Morning After the Win
Day 3: Writing Your Own Orders
Day 4: Prayer After the Loss
Day 5: Buried in the Tent

The challenge this week is simple and hard. Name the one win, the one Ai, or the one prayer you have buried in your tent, and bring it into the light before God has to deal with the whole camp. Pray before the wall falls, not after.

See you in the devos.
