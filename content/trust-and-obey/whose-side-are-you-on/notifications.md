WEEK OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This week we are standing in a field outside Jericho with Joshua and asking the same question he asked: whose side are You on? It turns out that is the wrong question. Day 1 opens with the most natural prayer in the world and shows you why God refuses to answer it. Day 2 shows what He says instead, and it reframes everything you thought you understood about how He moves.

Day 3 takes you into the names. There are two Joshuas in this field, one face down, one with a sword. The name on both of them means the same thing. Day 4 is the hardest day of the week: the boots come off. Worship is not what you bring to God. It is what you lay down. Day 5 lands the whole week on this: the battle plan is in chapter 6. The surrender is in chapter 5. You do not get one without the other.

See you in the devos.

Day 1 — The Wrong Question
Joshua walked up to the living God with his side already chosen. Most of us do the same thing every day. Devos are live.

Day 2 — He Came to Take Command
God's answer to Joshua's either/or was one word: No. Not for you. Not for them. Wrong question entirely. Devos are live.

Day 3 — Two Men Named Joshua
One Joshua face down in the dirt. One Joshua standing over him with a sword. Both names mean the same thing. Devos are live.

Day 4 — Take the Boots Off
The sandal is the manufactured thing you slide between yourself and the ground God formed you from. Take it off. Devos are live.

Day 5 — The Plan Is in the Next Chapter
The battle plan isn't in chapter 5. God gives blueprints to the man on his face, not the man still standing. Devos are live.
