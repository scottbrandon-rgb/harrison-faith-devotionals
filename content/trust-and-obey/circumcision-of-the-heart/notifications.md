## Series Announcement

This week in Trust & Obey we step into Joshua 5. The land is in front of Israel, and the Lord stops everything and says, "Pick up a knife." Before the conquest, He calls for the cut. Five days on what the Lord is asking you to surrender so the Commander of His army can show up at your wall.

Day 1: Pick Up the Knife
Day 2: What You Complain About
Day 3: Handed Down
Day 4: Today I Have Rolled It Away
Day 5: Now I Have Come
