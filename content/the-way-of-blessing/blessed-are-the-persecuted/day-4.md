## Devotional

At the center of persecution is not just what you believe—but who you belong to.

Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego didn’t stir up trouble. They didn’t start a fight. All they did was refuse to bow. The fire wasn’t about their attitude—it was about their allegiance.

That’s what persecution targets, loyalty. And when you’re truly aligned with Jesus, that loyalty is going to stand out. You don’t even have to be loud about it. Your obedience will offend those who bow to culture. And here’s the hard part: when you refuse to bow, you often become a threat to those who do.

The enemy doesn’t just want you to disbelieve—he wants you to compromise. Because if he can’t get you to quit on God, he’ll try to convince you to water Him down. But here’s the line in the sand:

“Even if He doesn’t deliver us… we won’t bow.”

That’s where you know peace has taken root. Not peace with the world—but peace with the Lord you serve. If your life belongs to Jesus, then there will come a moment where you must stand for Him. And that moment won’t feel comfortable—it will feel costly. But that cost is a confirmation.

You are not the first to face pressure for your convictions. And you won’t be the last. But don’t back down. You’re not just standing in a crowd—you’re standing in Christ. And He will walk with you through every fire.

## Application

1. Where are you being tempted to bow to pressure or blend in with culture? What would it look like to draw a line today—not in pride, but in loyalty? Who around you needs courage to stand? How could you encourage them?

## Challenge

Write out the words of Daniel 3:18: “But if not… we will not bow.”Keep it in your pocket or phone today as your quiet declaration of allegiance.

## Prayer

Jesus, give me the strength to stand. Not with arrogance, but with conviction. Let my loyalty to You be stronger than my desire for comfort. Remind me that You walk with those who walk through fire. And even if the answer doesn’t come the way I want, let it be known—I will not bow. In Your name, Amen.
