WEEK OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Most of us don't wake up one morning and decide to be far from God. We drift. One patch of grass at a time, one distraction at a time, until the shepherd's voice sounds faint and we've called the distance normal. This week's series, Counted, walks through the parable of the lost sheep and asks a question that might make you uncomfortable: are you lost and don't even know it?

We start with the shepherd counting and realizing one is missing, then move into the reality that sheep don't know when they're lost, they just normalize it. Day 3 confronts the hard truth that you are defenseless apart from the shepherd, you cannot save yourself no matter how hard you try. Day 4 takes us to the moment the shepherd lifts the sheep onto his shoulders, because you're not led home, you're carried. And we finish the week with the joy that drove Christ to endure the cross, the joy of bringing you back to the fold.

This week's challenge is simple: stop pretending you've got it handled and let the shepherd find you. See you in the devos.
